    • ROUND CREATION TOOL

      • Our Designers wanted a tool to edit rounds with these functions:

      • Rounds with scaling difficulty
      • Waves in those rounds
      • Show popup text (tutorials, hints)
      • Set/Pause/stop rounds and waves(save points)
      • Play sounds
        • At Round Start
        • At Wave Start
        • At difficulty change
        • At any time (To indicate a boss fight)
      • Spawn Enemies
        • At what spawnpoint?
        • What type of enemy?
        • How many?
        • Should the next wave wait to spawn until these enemies are dead?
    • Ai Implementation

      • We had 3 enemy concepts with different combat patterns and behaviors. I made the creation of the behaviors that would be universal a priority, to open up the doors for other programmers to jump on the ship with the creation of more specific behaviors.
      They were also gifted with an Attack System with support for multiple attacks/abilities depending on external data. This allowed us to use the same code for all of our units and the use of a specific ability class when needed.
      I made the Ai Navigation slot based to avoid to many enemies grouping up on the player at the same time, we applied this system to everything the Ai could interact with to stop odd scenarios.
